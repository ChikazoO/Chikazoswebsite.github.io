This is who
I am

I am ex-banker turned UX designer. I was born and raised in Lagos, Nigeria, but currently live in Germany. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Language and Linguistics. I always wanted to travel abroad and explore. Well, I eventually did and soon discovered UX design and with everything I had learnt about it I decided it was time for a career change.

Prior to discovering design, I worked with two of the top 5 banks in Nigeria. My Customer service background gave me the insight into understanding how to approach the emotional and behavioural processes of people from all walks of life. I worked directly with customers resolving their banking transaction issues, assisting them in using the banking apps and most importantly, empathizing with them when they had issues with their transactions. I thrived in my career because of my ability to ask the right questions that uncovered user problems in order to arrive at the most overarching solutions.

These skills have definitely been very useful in my journey as a UX Designer as it has allowed me to craft products that engage users in a way that is able to change perspectives and user experiences for the better. Now, I am looking for a role that allows me to use my enthusiasm in exploring and uncovering facts while collaborating with other UX designers to expand my expertise in the industry.

I enjoy travelling, spending time with my family, hanging out and having great conversations with friends.

Fun fact about me: I love to take selfies.
